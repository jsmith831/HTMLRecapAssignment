Covering the Basics

Woodworking is a fun way to work with your hands while creating things for other people to enjoy.

Below you will find simple steps to create great things all while being safe

  • Familiarize yourself with your work station
  • Wear the appropriate PPE
  • Always let others know before starting
  • Inspect tools and saw blades before beginning your project
